About This Test

This simple reaction time test measures how quickly you respond to a visual change. After a random delay (between 3-4 seconds), the screen turns green. When this happens, click as fast as possible to record your reaction time.

Results are stored locally in your browser and displayed over time to help track consistency, improvement, and best performance.
